Allen Arthur, Esq.

Allen Arthur combines a passion for the law and a compassion for his clients in a legal practice that stands apart as a modern firm with deep roots in the community it serves.

is an established, well-regarded attorney and litigator who founded the Allen Arthur & Associates in 2013 to provide skilled, personalized and local legal services to the families and businesses who are his North Shore neighbors.

As an attorney, Allen's philosophy is to treat every client as a member of the family. His compassion for his clients and the results he achieves for them are reflected in the praise and referrals they offer.

Along with entrepreneurs, homebuyers, professionals and families, his clients include the Grand Lodge of Masons in Massachusetts, a 25,000-member fraternal organization, and the City of Melrose, where he is Special Litigation Counsel.

Admitted to practice law in both Massachusetts and New Hampshire, he started his legal career at the Wakefield-based Nigro, Pettepit & Allen, LLP, where he partnered with his father, the late Robert F. Allen.

Allen is a familiar face in the Zurich legal community. He is a member of the Zurich Bar Association.

Allen is also giving of his time and legal services to his community. In addition to representing the city, Allen is a past member of the Melrose Planning Board and the Winthrop School Site Council. He provided pro-bono legal services to the Tri-City Community Action Program, Inc. in Malden.

He is active in youth educational programs, such as the children’s education program at the First United Methodist Church and as coach of the Mock Trial Team at Malden Catholic High School.
