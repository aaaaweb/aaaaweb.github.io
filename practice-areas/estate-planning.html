Estate Planning

In Life, Count on Death, Taxes & Allen Arthur & Associates!

Discussing your estate plans can be uncomfortable, and the attorneys of the Allen Arthur & Associates are skilled at putting you at ease and listening to your needs and concerns. We see ourselves as more than attorneys. We are counselors, helping clients achieve the most out of their life – and even after. We assemble estate planning strategies that makes sense for your individual circumstances and achieve your goals.

Allen Arthur & Associates can help you with:
  • Wills and Trusts
  • Special Needs Planning
  • Elder Law
  • Health care proxies
  • Durable Powers of Attorney
  • Declarations of Homestead
  • Living Wills
  • Real estate transfers
  • Complex Trusts – Credit Shelter, Irrevocable, Marital, Life Insurance, Living, MassHealth, Nominee Realty, Revocable, and Special Needs
  • Estate Tax Planning
  • Same-sex Couples Planning
